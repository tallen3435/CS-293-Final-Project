About UW-Green Bay

UW-Green Bay is a public university located in Green Bay, Wisconsin. Known for its commitment to sustainability, innovation, and academic excellence, the university offers a dynamic learning environment with a strong connection to the community and region.

Academics

UW-Green Bay offers more than 100 academic programs across four campuses. Students can pursue degrees in business, engineering, education, arts, and sciences, among many other areas.
